About Us

We offer high quality bespoke custom carpets as per your requirements. We have worked on numerous hospitality, hotel, airport and villa and palace projects, and we collaborate with some of the most famous designers in the world. Our services include measurements, custom fit-outs, wall-to-wall carpeting, bespoke designing and installation services on request.

Introduction

achal amit & Company was established in 1946 as a manufacturer of Carpets and Rugs and started it's exports from 1975. Since then it has never looked back and the company’s progress graph has seen a rising trend. The company has registered multifold growth specifically during the last 3 decades and has achieved a remarkable status in the international market.
